Chocolate Chip Cookie Reciepe

Chef:Suman

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Salt 1 tsp
Butter 1 cup
Granulated Suger 3/4 cup
Brown Suger 3/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Eggs 2
Chocolate chips 2 cups

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter, sugars, and vanilla until creamy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Cool on wire racks and enjoy!

Tips and Best Practices

  • Use room-temperature butter for better texture.
  • Chill dough for 30 mins before baking to prevent spreading.
  • Use parchment paper for easy cleanup.
  • Don't overmix the dough.
  • Store in an airtight container for freshness.
